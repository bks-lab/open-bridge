# Support

open-bridge is **open source (MIT) and maintained by one person**, best-effort, no SLA.
Here's the fastest way to get help — and what to honestly expect.

## Questions & how-to

- **"How do I…?" / "Is this possible?"** → open a
  [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/bks-lab/open-bridge/discussions) (Q&A).
- **Read first:** the [README](README.md), the live
  [overview page](https://bks-lab.github.io/open-bridge/), and [`docs/`](docs/)
  (feature tour, structure, extension model).
- **Want to build something for it?** → [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Bugs & feature requests

- Open an [issue](https://github.com/bks-lab/open-bridge/issues/new/choose) with a template.
- Skim [existing issues](https://github.com/bks-lab/open-bridge/issues) first — yours may be known.

## Security

- **Do not** open a public issue for a vulnerability. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
  (private reporting).

## What to expect

This is a young project — newly public, no external users yet, run by a solo maintainer.
Replies are best-effort and can take a while. A clear, reproducible report (or a PR) is the
fastest path to a fix. Thanks for your patience.
